Excellent Climbers

LEOPARD

Leopards are one of the most adaptable big cat species and are found in a wide range of habitats, from forests and grasslands to mountains and deserts, across Africa and parts of Asia.Leopards are good swimmers and are not averse to taking a dip, especially when they need to cross rivers or streams.

Tallest Land Animals

GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest terrestrial animals on Earth, with adult males reaching heights of up to 18 feet (5.5 meters) and females slightly shorter.Their iconic long necks can measure up to 6 feet (1.8 meters) in length. Despite their length, giraffes have the same number of neck vertebrae as humans, typically seven.

Bamboo Specialists

PANDA

Giant pandas are easily recognized by their black and white fur. They have black patches around their eyes, over their ears, and on their limbs, while the rest of their body is white.Pandas are primarily herbivores, with bamboo making up over 99% of their diet. They can eat a variety of bamboo species, but their favorite is the arrow bamboo.
